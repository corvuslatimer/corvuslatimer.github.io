Submission Rules

→AI or real life, both accepted. Generate with HeyGen, Runway, Kling, Pika, or shoot it yourself. Method doesn't matter — quality does.

→Follow the script. Characters, setting, and emotional beat should match the scene doc in the repo. Improvisation is fine if it serves the scene.
→One rejection = one revision allowed. If your submission doesn't pass, you get one chance to revise and resubmit before the scene is reopened.
